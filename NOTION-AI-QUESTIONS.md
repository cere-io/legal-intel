# Questions for Notion AI

Ask these in Notion AI to validate and improve assumptions. Organized by category.

---

## MARKET VALIDATION

1. "What is the current market size for eDiscovery tools and what percentage of law firms with fewer than 50 attorneys use any eDiscovery platform?"
2. "How many multi-jurisdictional crypto fraud cases are currently active in US federal courts? What's the typical duration and cost?"
3. "What are the top 5 complaints lawyers have about Relativity, Everlaw, and other eDiscovery platforms?"
4. "What is the SEC whistleblower program's total award payout to date, and how many submissions does it receive annually?"
5. "How do plaintiffs' firms currently coordinate evidence across multiple victims in RICO and mass fraud cases? What tools do they use?"
6. "What is the False Claims Act (qui tam) total recovery amount since 1986, and what percentage of cases are intervened by DOJ?"
7. "How many law firms have banned or restricted use of general-purpose AI (ChatGPT, Claude) due to privilege concerns?"
8. "What happened with Relativity's on-premises product? Is it still available or being sunset?"
9. "What are the GDPR implications for European law firms using US-hosted eDiscovery platforms like Relativity or Everlaw?"
10. "How do government prosecutors and JAG lawyers currently handle evidence management? What tools are available for classified or CJIS environments?"
